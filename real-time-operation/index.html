      Real time drilling Parameter

      Vital equipment in the drilling industry, providing real-time information from the drilling well. Real-time drilling parameters are data obtained and monitored continuously during the drilling process. This data includes several important factors that provide a deep understanding of the well’s condition and drilling operation at that time.

      Real time Trajectory Display

      The technology that enables users to monitor and visualize the trajectory of a drilling well directly during the drilling process. This is typically done with the assistance of specialized software that receives data from sensors in the field and projects it into easily understandable graphical representations.

      Real time Mudlog Interpretasi

      The process of analyzing and interpreting drilling mud data directly during the drilling process. This is carried out by geological and petrophysical experts working in real-time drilling monitoring units.

      Real Time Drilling Plan Vs Actual

      Involves continuous monitoring of drilling progress and comparing it to pre-established plans. This entails evaluating drilling parameters such as penetration rate, mud pressure, well temperature, and planned drilling trajectory

      History data

      Historical data in the context of well drilling can encompass various types of information recorded from previous drilling operations. This includes geological, technical, operational, and safety data relevant to the drilling process.

      Hardware Overview

      Canbus

      To perform the process of data acquisition from analog to digital signals directly related to sensors, we utilize hardware known as a CANbus. This hardware serves as a protector against electrical current spikes to the computerized system, ensuring resilience in the face of power surges. Moreover, the CANbus system boasts a high level of accuracy, reaching up to 99.9%, enhancing the precision and reliability of the data conversion process.

      Cancard

      To read the digital signals transmitted by the CANbus, we employ hardware known as the “Cancard.” This component plays a crucial role in expediting the sensor reading process, enhancing its efficiency and accuracy. The Cancard is specifically designed to optimize the retrieval of digital signals from the CANbus, ensuring a swift and precise data acquisition experience.
