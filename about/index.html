The reliability and proven performance of SHENKAI products align with our commitment to providing high-quality and efficient solutions in the realm of drilling operations.

      About Our Company

      PT Indra Petrotech Indonesia is a company operating in the drilling sector in Indonesia, where our primary focus lies in leasing Mud Logging Units (MLU) along with experienced professionals with more than 5 years of expertise. The MLU is an instrument utilized to record, monitor, and evaluate all activities and data occurring during drilling operations. In addition to MLU, we also offer various services, such as Real-Time Operation. This is a web-based application capable of displaying real-time/online drilling parameter data along with live CCTV feeds from a drilling project. The application is easily accessible through laptops or smartphones
